Why Should You Clear Photo Metadata?

When you take a photo with a smartphone or digital camera, it saves hidden information called EXIF data. This includes your exact GPS coordinates, date, time, and device serial numbers. Sharing these images on classified sites (like Craigslist or eBay), forums, or via email can accidentally leak your home address and daily routines to strangers.

Why Our Metadata Remover is Safer

Unlike other online tools, our Free EXIF Cleaner works entirely on the client side. Your photos are never uploaded to any external server. The metadata extraction and removal process happen right inside your web browser. You can even use this tool completely offline!
